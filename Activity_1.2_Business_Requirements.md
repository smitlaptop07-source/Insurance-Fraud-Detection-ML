# Activity 1.2 : Business Requirements

## Requirements

- Accurate Detection: The model must accurately classify claims as
  fraudulent or legitimate with high precision and recall.

- Scalability: The system should handle large volumes of insurance
  claims data efficiently.

- Speed: Fraud detection must happen in real-time or near real-time
  to prevent delayed claim processing.

- Compliance: The solution must comply with data privacy laws and
  insurance regulations.

- User-Friendly Interface: A simple web interface for insurance
  officers to input claim details and get fraud predictions.

- Explainability: The model should provide reasons for flagging
  a claim as fraudulent.

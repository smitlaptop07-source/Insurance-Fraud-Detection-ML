# Activity 1.1 : Specify the Business Problem

## Problem Statement
Insurance fraud is a major financial threat to insurance companies worldwide.
Fraudulent claims cost the industry billions of dollars every year.
Manually detecting fraud is slow, expensive, and prone to human error.

## Objective
Build a Machine Learning model that automatically detects whether an
insurance claim is fraudulent or legitimate based on historical claim data.

## Goal
- Reduce financial losses due to fraudulent claims
- Speed up the fraud detection process
- Improve accuracy compared to manual detection

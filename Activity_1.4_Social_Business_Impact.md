# Activity 1.4 : Social or Business Impact

## Business Impact
- Saves insurance companies millions of dollars annually
- Speeds up genuine claim approvals
- Reduces operational costs of manual fraud investigation
- Improves profitability and sustainability of insurance firms

## Social Impact
- Honest policyholders benefit from faster claim processing
- Reduces insurance premium costs for genuine customers
- Builds trust between customers and insurance companies
- Helps regulators monitor fraudulent activities in the industry

## Future Scope
- Integration with real-time claim processing systems
- Use of additional data sources like social media and telematics
- Continuous model retraining with new fraud patterns
